*Greek actress (1942–2017)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Zoe Laskari][1]

Zoe Laskari was a Greek actress and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Star Hellas 1959 and represented her country at Miss Universe 1959 where she placed Top 15.
