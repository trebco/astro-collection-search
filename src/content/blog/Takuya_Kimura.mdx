*Japanese actor, singer, and radio personality (born 1972)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Takuya Kimura][1]

Takuya Kimura  is a Japanese actor, singer, and radio personality. He is regarded as a Japanese icon after achieving success as an actor. He was also a popular member of SMAP, one of the best-selling boy bands in Asia. In the media, he is known as an huge heartthrob in Japan, and a sex symbol. In April 1988, producer Johnny Kitagawa chose six out of the twenty boys to create a new boy band; "SMAP."
