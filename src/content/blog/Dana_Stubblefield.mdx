*American football player (born 1970)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Dana Stubblefield][1]

Dana William Stubblefield is an American former professional football player. He was a defensive tackle in the National Football League (NFL). After graduating from Taylor High School in North Bend, Ohio, Stubblefield attended the University of Kansas. He is serving a sentence of 15 years to life in prison, after being convicted of rape.
