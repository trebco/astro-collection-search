*Duchess of Bedford*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Anne of Burgundy][1]

Anne of Burgundy, Duchess of Bedford was a daughter of John the Fearless, Duke of Burgundy (1371–1419), and his wife Margaret of Bavaria (1363–1423).
