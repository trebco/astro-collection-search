*Grand Prince of Vladimir from 1252 to 1263*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Alexander Nevsky][1]

Alexander Yaroslavich Nevsky was Prince of Novgorod, Grand Prince of Kiev (1246–1263) and Grand Prince of Vladimir (1252–1263).
