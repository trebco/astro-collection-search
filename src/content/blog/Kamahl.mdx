*Australian singer and entertainer (born 1934)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Kamahl][1]

Kandiah Kamalesvaran, better known by his stage name Kamahl, is an Australian singer and recording artist. He has been in the Australian music industry over for 70 years and has made some memorable TV and film appearances, as well as concerts.
