*American actor*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Cory Michael Smith][1]

Cory Michael Smith is an American actor. He appeared in 2013 in Breakfast at Tiffany's on Broadway, which starred Emilia Clarke. He has also appeared in several of Todd Haynes's films, including Carol (2015), Wonderstruck (2017), and May December (2023).
