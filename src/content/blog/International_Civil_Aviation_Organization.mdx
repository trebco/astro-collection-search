*Specialised agency of the United Nations*  
Source (Wikipedia): [International Civil Aviation Organization][1]

The International Civil Aviation Organization is a specialized agency of the United Nations that coordinates the principles and techniques of international air navigation, and fosters the planning and development of international air transport to ensure safe and orderly growth.
