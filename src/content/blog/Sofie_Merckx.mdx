*Belgian politician (born 1974)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Sofie Merckx][1]

Sofie Merckx is a Belgian physician, politician and member of the Chamber of Representatives. A member of the Workers' Party of Belgium, she has represented Hainaut since June 2019.
