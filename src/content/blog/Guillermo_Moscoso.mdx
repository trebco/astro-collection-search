*Venezuelan baseball player (born 1983)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Guillermo Moscoso][1]

Guillermo Alejandro Moscoso is a Venezuelan former professional baseball pitcher. He has previously played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Texas Rangers, Oakland Athletics, Colorado Rockies and San Francisco Giants.
