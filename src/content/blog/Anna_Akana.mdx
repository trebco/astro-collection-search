*American actress and filmmaker (born 1989)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Anna Akana][1]

Anna Kay Napualani Akana is an American actress, comedian, filmmaker, musician, and YouTuber. She has appeared in TV series, films, and music videos that include Awkward (2011), Ant-Man (2015), Hello, My Name is Doris (2016), Dirty 30 (2016), and Big City Greens (2018).
