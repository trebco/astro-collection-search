*American actor*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Graham Patrick Martin][1]

Graham Patrick Martin is an American actor. He is best known for his recurring role as Eldridge on Two and a Half Men (2010–12) and former teen hustler Rusty Beck on the series finale of The Closer and in its spinoff series, Major Crimes (2012–2018). Martin also portrayed Bill Engvall's older son in the sitcom The Bill Engvall Show (2007–09).
He starred in the films Jack Ketchum's The Girl Next Door (2007) and Rising Stars (2010).
