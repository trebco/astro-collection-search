*Paraguayan footballer (born 1984)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Lucas Barrios][1]

Lucas Ramón Barrios Cáceres is a Paraguayan professional footballer who plays as a forward. Barrios is known for his effectiveness in the target area. This earned him his nickname La Pantera, which means The Panther.
