*Dutch politician (1931–2010)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Hans van Mierlo][1]

Henricus Antonius Franciscus Maria Oliva "Hans" van Mierlo was a Dutch politician and journalist who co-founded Democrats 66 (D66).
