*American baseball player (born 1999)*  
Source (Wikipedia): [Brett Baty][1]

Brett Austin Baty is an American professional baseball third baseman for the New York Mets of Major League Baseball (MLB). The Mets selected Baty in the first round of the 2019 MLB draft. He made his MLB debut in 2022.
